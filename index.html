ABOUT

Our goal is to create the world strongest and more prosperous community of companies, young entrepreneurs, governments and first-time workers around a common goal: financial prosperity and teamwork. We are a company operating in five countries in the Americas dedicated to the exchange and international promotion of talent and ideas. We conceive a future where everyone can dedicate their live to what truly drives them forward.

WHAT WE DO

We develop programs around entrepreneurship, innovation and financial inclusion in the countries we attend. We also help our clients achieve the 2030 Sustainable Development Goals number 4 “Quality Education” and 8 “Decent Work and Economic Growth”.

Crowdfunding

Through our online piggy bank we provide a debt-free financial tool to support university student projects and develop an international network of young creators. Our goal is to launch 1,000 new campaigns and raise 1M USD by 2023.

With our programs, we link private sector companies and government agencies with those innovative ideas they might be needing. By doing so, we can also involve more than 20,000 young entrepreneurs and first-time workers in our opportunity creation programs.
